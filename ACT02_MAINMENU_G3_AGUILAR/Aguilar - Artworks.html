MY 3 POSTER ARTWORKS

......

1. EQUALITY

Date: March 17, 2018

Short Description: This poster depicts equality as it should be. A man and a woman holding together a torch of equality on its road to the current era where technology is very dominant. On its sides represent thing that male people achieved which women can do too like graduationg, becomaing who they want to be and even praying because praying is a very genderless thing to do.

2. FAMILY

Date: October 22, 2019

Short Description: This poster depicts a lovely family as it should be. A man and a woman holding together a flag of Philippines and a house on top to symbolize the humble homes of each family. On its sides represents the responsibilities that parents do have like making their children eat, study and more. Ontop of the house is a bird that symbolizes peace holding a cross symbolizing God to represent the peace in each of our homes.

3. PARENT'S LOVE

Date: February 04, 2018

Short Description: This poster depicts how big our mother/parents has contributed to our life. A woman, our parents, holding a globe which represents the world uniting all womens, parents and mothers all over the world, on top of it is a torch and a road leading to success and peace indicating that when we love our parents, success shall follow.
